The E-Commerce website is used to solve the problem of people not being able to find items in stores available. It provides an easy way to sell products to a large customer base. Most online market platforms have a “fixed price” policy, which takes away one of the most important features of commerce - the ability to negotiate prices. With the element of bargaining, a product can be bought for less than its initial listed price. Adding a negotiation feature would innovate online marketplaces, be seen as a major benefit to many consumers, and undoubtedly increase e-commerce use.
